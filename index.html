Toucan Bills

**Key Idea:** Toucan bills are very light but very strong.

**Human Connection:** Scientists used this idea to design safer car bumpers.

**Discussion:** How is the toucan bill like a car bumper? How is it different?

Shark Scales

**Key Idea:** Shark scales make them fast in the water.

**Human Connection:** Engineers used this idea to design faster swimsuits and aircraft.

**Discussion:** How do shark scales help them move? How does this idea help humans?
